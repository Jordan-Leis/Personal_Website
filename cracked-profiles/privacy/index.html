Cracked-Profiles Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-08

Cracked-Profiles (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a community voting app where users submit their professional profile for ranking and can vote on head-to-head matchups.

What we collect

  • Account info from sign-in (e.g., name and email, depending on what the provider returns).
  • Profile submission data you provide: LinkedIn profile URL and manually-entered metrics (e.g., follower count, experience count).
  • Interests you choose (e.g., SWE, research, growth).
  • Voting activity: votes you cast and matchup results.
  • Ratings data: baseline MMR and current MMR computed from your submitted metrics and vote outcomes.
  • Basic logs for security and debugging (e.g., request timestamps, errors).

What we do not do

  • We do not scrape LinkedIn pages.
  • We do not access “analytics” for other LinkedIn profiles.
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not run third-party ad tracking.

How we use data

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your submitted profile card in matchups and leaderboards.
  • To compute ratings (MMR/Elo) and generate matchups.
  • To prevent abuse (rate limits, duplicate vote detection, and security checks).

Where data is stored / processed

The app may be hosted using services such as Vercel (web hosting) and Supabase/Postgres (database & authentication). These providers process data on our behalf to operate the service.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the app. You can request deletion at any time (see below).

Deletion

You can delete your account and associated data from within the app (if available), or by contacting us. Deletion is intended to be a hard delete (not a “soft delete”).
